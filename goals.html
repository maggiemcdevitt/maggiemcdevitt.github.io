Goal Setting

My key to staying motivated day-to-day and month-by-month is by setting goals. My goals are both short and long term, but if I don't have an attainable plan as to how I will achieve them, I won't succeed. The key to following through and achieving your goals is to set yourself up for success and make a detailed plan.

As of March 2023, these are the current goals that I have been working towards:

Staying active
Each week I've gone to between two and four Dragonfly Yoga classes. I typically go to variations of their Flow 60 and barre classes (both hot), and it always is one of the best parts of my day. I also try to always get at least 10,000 steps in a day, which is easier some days than others depending on my class schedule.
Stay on top of my work
This semester has definitely been my hardest, especially because I recently started a job tutoring Chemistry through UW Athletics. I have had to make more of an effort to wake up early to get done everything I want to in a day, as well as making the most of my weekends to catch up and get ahead. A great tool for me this semester has been using Google Calendar. It helps me to keep all of my meetings, due dates, and work schedule straight.
Quality time with friends
Maintaining a healthy balance in my life and having fun/spending time with my friends is essential for my mental health
